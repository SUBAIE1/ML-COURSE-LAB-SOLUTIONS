## 1.Problem statement 

### 1.1:Context

The selected dataset is obtained from kaggle in CSV form represnting customer information collected in a retail environment.It contains demographic and behavioral data related to customers.


### 1.2:Problem

Businesses are aiming to augment a clear picture of customrs spending behavior in order to improve their marketing and enhance strategies of customers engagement.This dataset present the best aspects of that objective, the reason is it contains the major features that are essential to fulfil the problem's requieremnts.For example, Predicting how much a customer is likely to spend based on their visit behavior can support better decision-making.



### 1.3:Data Description

The dataset is tabular and contains six features.Four numerical attributes: age, annual income, spending score, and monthly visits; and two categorical attributes: gender and preffered product

### 1.4:ML Model 

The problem is defined as a supervised regression task. The dataset contains a continuous variable called spending score, which will serve as the target variable (y).

The goal is to build a regression model that learns the relationship between customersâ€™ monthly visits ( input feature - x ) and their spending score (target variable). The model is expected to predict the spending score of customers based on how frequently they visit the store.
